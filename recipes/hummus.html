Hummus Recipe

Description:

Today we are making the Hummus, a very popular dish that originates from the middle-eastern countries


Ingredients:

  • 2 cloves garlic

  • 1 (19 ounce) can garbanzo beans, half the liquid reserved

  • 4 tablespoons lemon juice

  • 2 tablespoons tahini

  • 1 teaspoon salt

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • Black pepper to taste
