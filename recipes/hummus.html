Hummus Recipe

Description:

Today we are making the Hummus, a very popular dish that originates from the middle-eastern countries


Ingredients:

  • 2 cloves garlic

  • 1 (19 ounce) can garbanzo beans, half the liquid reserved

  • 4 tablespoons lemon juice

  • 2 tablespoons tahini

  • 1 teaspoon salt

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • Black pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Chop garlic in a blender.

  2. Pour garbanzo beans into the blender, reserving about 1 tablespoon for garnish.
    Add reserved liquid, lemon juice, tahini, and salt to the blender.
    Blend until creamy and well mixed.

  3. Transfer mixture to a medium serving bowl. Drizzle olive oil and sprinkle pepper over the top.
    Garnish with reserved garbanzo beans.

  4. Enjoy!
