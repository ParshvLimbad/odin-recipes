Cheese Sticks Recipe

Description:

Mozzarella sticks are very easy to make at home. They're deep-fried until golden and crispy on the outside,
with melted gooey cheese on the inside. They're so good and take just minutes to fry! Try dipping them in a marinara sauce!


Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs, beaten

  • ¼ cup water

  • 1 ½ cups Italian seasoned bread crumbs

  • ½ teaspoon garlic salt

  • ⅔ cup all-purpose flour

  • ⅓ cup cornstarch

  • 2 cups oil for frying, or as needed

  • 1 (16 ounce) package mozzarella cheese sticks
